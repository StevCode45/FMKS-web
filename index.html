Our Beautiful Campus

Our History

Founded in 2010, Future Model Of Knowledge School (FMKS) began with a vision to integrate Islamic education with modern academic excellence. Starting with 80 students, we have grown into a comprehensive Islamic educational institution serving over 800 students from nursery through secondary school.

Our journey has been blessed with consistent achievements in both Islamic studies and conventional subjects. Our students excel in WAEC, NECO, and JAMB examinations while maintaining strong Islamic values and knowledge of Quran, Hadith, and Arabic language.

Our Achievements

  • 95% WAEC pass rate
  • Best Islamic School in Lagos 2023
  • National Quran Recitation Champions
  • 80+ students memorized full Quran
  • Excellence in Arabic Language Award

Our Vision

To be the leading Islamic educational institution in Nigeria, producing globally competitive Muslim graduates who excel academically while maintaining strong Islamic values and character.

Our Mission

To provide quality Islamic education that integrates Quranic teachings, modern academics, and character development, nurturing well-rounded Muslims prepared for both worldly success and the hereafter.

Our Curriculum

We integrate the Nigerian National Curriculum with comprehensive Islamic studies including Quran memorization, Arabic language, Islamic history, and Hadith studies alongside modern subjects.

Admissions

Join our community of learners and begin your journey to academic excellence.

Admission Requirements

Primary School (Nursery - Primary 6)

  • Birth certificate
  • Immunization record
  • Passport photographs (4 copies)
  • Previous school report (if applicable)
  • Entrance assessment for Primary 2 and above

Secondary School (JSS 1 - SS 3)

  • Primary School Leaving Certificate
  • Birth certificate
  • Passport photographs (6 copies)
  • Previous school testimonial
  • Entrance examination (English, Mathematics, Basic Science)

Admission Process

1

Application Form

Complete and submit the admission form with required documents.

2

Entrance Assessment

Take the entrance examination or assessment (age-appropriate).

3

Interview

Parent and student interview with the admissions committee.

4

Admission Decision

Receive admission decision within 2 weeks of assessment.

5

Enrollment

Complete enrollment by paying fees and submitting final documents.

Important Dates

  • Application Opens: January 15th
  • Application Deadline: March 30th
  • Entrance Exams: April 15th - 30th
  • Admission Results: May 15th
